ACME CORPORATION — NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING CHECKLIST
Document ID: HR-ON-001 | Last Updated: February 2026 | Owner: Noah Anderson (HR BP)

Welcome to Acme! This checklist covers everything you need to do in your
first 30 days. Your manager and HR Business Partner will guide you through each step.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
BEFORE YOUR START DATE (HR / IT WILL SEND YOU)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  □ Welcome email with laptop shipping info (if remote) or office pickup location
  □ Workday login link — complete I-9 and direct deposit before Day 1
  □ Background check clearance confirmation
  □ Employee ID and badge access request (IT will action this)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
DAY 1
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  □ Receive laptop from IT (or confirm delivery) and power it on
  □ Complete initial macOS/Windows setup wizard
  □ Log in to your Acme Google Workspace account (@acmecorp.com)
  □ Set up Google Authenticator for MFA (required for all Acme systems)
  □ Install and configure Cisco AnyConnect VPN (see IT-RB-001)
  □ Install required software from the Acme App Catalog (catalog.acmecorp.com):
      - Slack (join workspace: acmecorp.slack.com)
      - Zoom
      - 1Password (your manager will share the vault invite)
  □ Join mandatory Slack channels: #general, #announcements, #your-team
  □ HR orientation call with Noah Anderson (30 min, calendar invite sent)
  □ 1:1 with your manager (1 hour — agenda: team intro, role expectations, 30-60-90)
  □ Complete "Security Awareness Training" in Workday (takes 45 min, due by Day 3)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WEEK 1 (DAYS 1–5)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  □ Submit benefits enrollment in Workday (deadline: Day 10)
  □ Set up your 401(k) contribution at fidelity.com/acme
  □ Complete I-9 employment eligibility (bring ID documents if in-office)
  □ Attend weekly team standup (your manager will add you)
  □ Meet with your onboarding buddy (assigned by HR — check Slack DM)
  □ Read the HR Policy Handbook (hr.acmecorp.com)
  □ Read the Security & Acceptable Use Policy (it.acmecorp.com/security-policy)
  □ Request system access by submitting helpdesk.acmecorp.com/access-request:
      - GitHub organization invite (Engineering only)
      - Salesforce (Sales/Marketing only)
      - BigQuery read-only (Analytics/Engineering)
      - Any team-specific tools (ask your manager)
  □ Shadow a teammate on a real task or project

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WEEK 2–4 (FIRST MONTH)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  □ Complete all required Workday training modules:
      - "Preventing Workplace Harassment" (due by Day 14)
      - "Data Privacy and GDPR Basics" (due by Day 14)
      - Role-specific compliance training (varies by team)
  □ Schedule 30-minute "coffee chats" with 5 colleagues outside your team
  □ Attend the monthly All-Hands meeting (first Tuesday of each month)
  □ Submit first expense report if applicable (via Expensify)
  □ Set up your development environment (Engineering only — see DEV-GUIDE-003)
  □ Complete your 30-day check-in with HR (Noah Anderson will reach out)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
30-DAY MILESTONE GOALS (DISCUSS WITH MANAGER)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  □ You understand your team's mission and OKRs
  □ You have shipped or contributed to at least one project/task
  □ You know who to ask for help in each major area
  □ You have set initial performance goals for your first 90 days in Workday
  □ You feel comfortable asking questions in Slack and raising blockers
